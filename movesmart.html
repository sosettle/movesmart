The Process

From data to your perfect match

We pull from five authoritative public datasets, engineer thematic profiles for every CBSA, and surface cities that match your preferences.

01

Data Collection

We aggregate indicators from the Census Bureau, FBI crime statistics, CDC health data, NOAA climate records, and EPA walkability scores.

02

Theme Engineering

Raw variables are standardized and grouped into six core themes — Affordability, Job Growth, Safety, Diversity, Urban Form, and Outdoor Lifestyle.

03

City Clustering

PCA + K-Means clustering reveals natural city archetypes — structural similarities that group metros into meaningful, interpretable clusters.

04

Personalized Matching

You adjust weights across the six themes. Our content-based recommendation engine ranks cities by how closely they match your profile.

05

AI City Summaries

An LLM reads your top matches and generates natural-language explanations — highlighting why each city fits your specific priorities.

What We Measure

Six dimensions of livability

Every metro area receives a score across these themes. You decide how much each one matters to you.

🏠

Affordability

How far does your dollar go? We measure housing costs relative to local incomes, factoring in rent burden and cost-of-living pressures.

Median home value Rent-to-income ratio Housing cost burden Median rent
📈

Job Growth

Economic opportunity and employment stability. Strong job markets offer more than just employment — they signal a city's trajectory.

Employment growth rate Unemployment rate Industry diversity
🛡️

Safety

Per-capita crime rates normalized for population density. Both violent and property crime contribute to the overall safety score.

Violent crime per capita Property crime per capita
🤝

Diversity

A quantified racial and ethnic diversity index that captures the richness and representation of communities across a metro area.

Racial diversity index Ethnic diversity index Educational attainment
🏙️

Urban Form

How dense, walkable, and connected is the city? Urban form shapes daily life — from your commute to spontaneous street-level encounters.

Population density Walkability score Housing density Land area
🏔️

Outdoor Lifestyle

Access to nature, protected lands, and climate comfort. For those who want the city and the wilderness within reach.

Distance to parks Protected land % Climate comfort Physical activity index

What's Built

Minimum Viable Product

Feature 01

Thematic City Profiles

Every CBSA receives a structured score across six themes, constructed from standardized multi-dataset indicators. The foundation for everything else.

Feature 02

Cluster Analysis

PCA dimensionality reduction followed by K-Means clustering groups metros into interpretable archetypes with dominant thematic signatures.

Feature 03

Recommendation Engine

Content-based filtering matches users to CBSAs via weighted distance scoring. Preference sliders translate to a ranked list with match scores.

Feature 04

LLM City Summaries

A large language model generates personalized natural-language summaries explaining why each recommended city aligns with your stated priorities.

Feature 05

Interactive Dashboard

A Streamlit application connects everything — preference controls, cluster visualizations, ranked recommendations, and AI-generated explanations in one place.
